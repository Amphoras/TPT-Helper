TPT Helper is designed for ZTE phones to make it easy to repartition and (for ZTE Blade) to convert your phone to run the latest custom roms.

The ZTE Blade Gen 1 to Gen 2 TPTs used were created by wbaw.

Moldovan Mile High Mountain Pie created by KonstaT.

Thanks to cpg for writing ztepack and for allowing me to use it.

Translations:
French translations by Alex131089.
German translations by FelixL.
Russian translations by top4ek.
Chinese translations by wuxipaopao.
Serbian translations by Sokobeg.

Auto TPT Mode:
Auto TPT mode is currently a work in progress. It is confirmed working on Ginger Stir Fry and CAE. It should also work on any ZTE rom or roms based on these (Swedish Spring etc.).

Currently it does not work on CyanogenMod 7 or GingerBlade.
